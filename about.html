My Coding Journey


I would definitely recommend the SheCodes Online Coding Workshops to women who wish start their coding journey. You will be challenged with different exercises and projects, but the concepts are thoroughly explained and a solution is provided for every exercise. The homework and deadlines are designed to test your knowledge and keep you focused. All in all, the workshops really helped me to put what I had learned into practice, whilst introducing me to new concepts.

My goal now is to work on real-world coding projects to expand my career opportunities and continue to grow as a Front-end Developer.
